Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

1. Information We Collect

AI Calc is a privacy-first application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All calculations and history are stored locally on your device using LocalStorage.

2. Local Storage

Your calculation history and theme preferences are saved locally on your browser/device. You can clear this data at any time using the "Clear" button in the Records section of the app.

3. Third-Party Services

The application uses the following third-party resources via CDN for functionality:

  • Tailwind CSS: For styling.
  • Font Awesome: For iconography.
  • Math.js: For complex mathematical processing.

These services do not have access to your private calculation data.

4. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer through the GitHub repository.
